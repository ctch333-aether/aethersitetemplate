B Corp Certification

Third-party certification measuring a company's entire social and environmental impact. AETHER maintains B Corp status, requiring vendors to meet rigorous standards of social and environmental performance, accountability, and transparency. Certification verified by B Lab.

Declare Label

A "nutrition label" for building products requiring manufacturers to disclose all ingredients. Part of the Living Building Challenge framework, ensuring material transparency across fabrication and venue operations. Products must list all ingredients down to 100ppm.

ILFI (International Living Future Institute)

Organization behind Living Building Challenge and Declare Labels. AETHER's primary sustainability certification partner for venue construction and materials procurement. Sets the world's most rigorous green building standards.

Living Building Challenge

ILFI's most ambitious performance standard for buildings. Requires net-positive energy, water, and waste performance. Guides AETHER venue development toward regenerative design principles.
